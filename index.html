Honeydukes Creamery Brief History

The owner's favorite comfort food is ice cream, out of passion, they've created their own version of the new ice cream trend, which is the rolled ice cream. Honeydukes Creamery was established on May 2018. Started at house garage and became trending after a week. As of today, Honeydukes is already known and became the favorite dessert of the locals and awarded as one of 2019 Best of the Best Products of La Union.
